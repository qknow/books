Land Use

Overlapping of types of resources

There are some types of resources which can be a combination of 2 or more types.

examples:

Apart from being a renewable resource (through hydroelectric energy), Water can also be considered as a recyclable resource since it goes through the process of precipitation and evaporation to be reused.

Trees (renewable resource) that cannot grow back on a piece of land due to farming activities can be classified as non-renewable resources. Trees are cleared to reduce competition for nutrients with crops.
