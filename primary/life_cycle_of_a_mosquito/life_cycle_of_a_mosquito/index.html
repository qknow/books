INTRODUCTION

Life Cycle of a Mosquito

  1. Egg: The mosquito lays egg on the surface of the water such as stagnant water or in a gutter.

  2. Lava: The egg then hatches into larva often three days.

  3. Pupa: The lava grows into pupa

  4. Adult mosquito: the pupa then grow to become an adult mosquito.
