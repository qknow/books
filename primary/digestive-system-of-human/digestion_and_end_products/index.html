INTRODUCTION

Digestion and end products

Every food has its site for digestion and end products. For example, rice will not stay the same when it enters the human body. The body must process the rice in order to take the nutrients in it for its activities. The rice will undergo digestion through the digestive system and after digestion, glucose is acquired from the rice. The glucose formed is the simplest unit that can be obtained from rice. Glucose is used by the body to perform its activities.

Let’s look at some foods, its digestion site and its end product.

NUTRIENTS FOOD DIGESTION SITE END PRODUCT
Carboohydrates Banku, Rice, Fufu, Bread, yam Mouth Glucose
Protein Beans, fish, meat Stomach Amino acid
Fat and oil Butter, palm oil, cheese Small Intestine Glycerol
