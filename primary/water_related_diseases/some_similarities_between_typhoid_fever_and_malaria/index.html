INTRODUCTION

Some similarities between typhoid fever and malaria

Typhoid fever and malaria show some similar symptoms. It is advised to be tested for the sickness before put on any medications. In the absence of verifiable laboratory test one can mistake the emerging symptoms of typhoid for malaria. Some of their similar symptoms are;

a. Fever

b. Cold

c. Headache

d. Loss of appetite

The malaria disease can be prevented by preventing its causative agent the mosquito. The breeding of mosquitoes results in malaria and there are a lot of conditions which promote the breeding of mosquitoes. They are;

a. Stagnant Water

b. Bushy Areas

c. Choked gutters
