Ways of preventing malaria

The malaria disease can be prevented by preventing its causative agent the mosquito. The breeding of mosquitoes results in malaria and there are a lot of conditions which promote the breeding of mosquitoes. They are;

a. Stagnant Water

b. Bushy Areas

c. Choked gutters
