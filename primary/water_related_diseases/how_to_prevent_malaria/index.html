INTRODUCTION

How to prevent malaria

Malaria can be prevented by stopping the breed of mosquitoes. These are;

a. Practice good sanitation

b. Desilting and cleaning of gutters

c. Preventing stagnant water

d. Weeding of bushy areas

The practices above will prevent the breed of mosquitoes. But there are instances whereby the mosquitoes already exist. There are ways to control them, these are;

  1. Use of mosquito insecticides

  1. Use of mosquito nets

  1. Introduction of fishes into mosquito breeding ponds to feed on the mosquitoes.
