Life Cycle of Okro and Maize Plants

Plants also called green plants are living things that grow in the soil. They obtain most of their energy from sunlight to undergo photosynthesis. Plants usually rely on soil for support and water. Plants undergo cycles in order to produce offspring and also to mature.
