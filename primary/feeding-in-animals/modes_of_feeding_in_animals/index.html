INTRODUCTION

Modes of feeding in animals

There are different modes of feeding in animals. Some animals feed on only herbs or plants, some feed on only animals and others feed on both. This type of feeding is what we refer to as mode of feeding.

We have three (3) modes of feeding. They are;

Herbivorous feeding: This is a mode of feeding where animals feed only on herbs or plants. Animals that feed on only plants and herbs are called herbivores.

Carnivorous feeding: This is a mode of feeding whereby animals feed on only animal’s flesh. Animals that feed on only animals are called carnivores.

Omnivorous Feeding: This is the mode of feeding whereby animals feed on both herbs or plants and animals. The animals that feed on both herbs or plants and animals flesh are called omnivores
